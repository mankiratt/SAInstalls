OUR STORY

30 years of craft.
A lifetime of dedication.

S&A Installs & Joinery was built on a simple belief — that every home deserves quality craftsmanship that stands the test of time. With over 30 years of personal experience and a combined 60 years between our team, we bring unmatched knowledge and skill to every project we take on.

Every piece we build is handcrafted to your exact specifications. We use only premium materials and hardware — including Blum and Hafele — because we believe the details matter. From the first consultation to the final installation, we treat every home like our own.

Based in Melbourne's south east, we service Clyde North, Berwick, Cranbourne, Narre Warren, Pakenham, Dandenong and surrounding suburbs.
